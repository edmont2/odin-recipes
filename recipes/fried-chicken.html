Fried Chicken

Fried chicken is just amazing! It can be eaten with anything and even by itself.

I usually prefer to accompany it with white rice and stewed beans.

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 or 1/2 of chicken
  • canola/vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp of seasoning (adobo and sazon)
  • 1 tbsp of garlic (ground or powder)

Steps:

  1. Marinate the chicken with the seasoning (adobo and sazon) and garlic.
  2. Add canola/vegetable oil into a pot, and heat it to medium high on your stove.
  3. Add the marinated chicken to the pot and cook on medium low until internal temperature of the chicken reaches 165 degrees F.
  4. Increase temperature to medium high in order to broil the chicken for a crispy finish.
  5. When the outer skin of the chicken has an orange golden color and it's crispy, they are ready to serve!
